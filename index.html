Zoning & Development

This property is zoned R-2 (Single-Family Attached Residential) under City of Commerce City regulations.

R-2 zoning is intended for residential development and typically allows for:

  • Duplex construction (two residential units)
  • Single-family attached housing
  • Standard residential improvements

This makes the lot well-suited for buyers looking to build a duplex or income-generating residential property.
